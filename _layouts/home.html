What to Expect

Your first visit is important to us — here’s what Sunday looks like

Warm Welcome

Friendly faces will greet you at the door. No dress code, no judgement — come exactly as you are.

Heartfelt Worship

Lively, Spirit-led worship with a mix of contemporary songs and timeless hymns that draw us closer to God.

Biblical Teaching

Practical, relevant messages grounded in Scripture that equip you for everyday life and deepen your faith.

Prayer & Support

Our prayer team is available every Sunday. Whatever you are going through, you do not have to face it alone.

Kids & Youth

Engaging, age-appropriate programmes for children and young people — so the whole family can grow in faith.

Multicultural Family

People from every background and nation, united in Christ. Our diversity is one of our greatest strengths.
