## Why Give?

Generosity is at the heart of what we believe. Everything we have is a gift from God, and giving is one of the ways we express our gratitude and our trust in him.

Your giving makes a real difference. It enables us to run our Sunday services, invest in children's and youth ministry, support The Well Centre's community work in Reading, and continue reaching people through our live-streamed services and daily devotionals online.

We have seen God provide faithfully, and we are grateful for every person who partners with us. Every gift — whatever its size — is received with genuine thankfulness.

> "Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."
>
> — 2 Corinthians 9:7 (NIV)

## How to Give

We want giving to be as simple as possible. To receive our bank details for a regular standing order or a one-off transfer, please [get in touch]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}) and we will send them to you directly.

**Email:** [life@thewell-church.com](mailto:life@thewell-church.com)

## Gift Aid

If you are a UK taxpayer, please consider **Gift Aid**. It allows us to reclaim 25p for every £1 you give at no extra cost to you. Just let us know when you get in touch and we will send you the simple declaration form.

## What Your Giving Supports

- **Sunday services** — Venue costs, equipment, and everything that goes into gathering each week
- **Children and youth** — Resources, activities, and staff to invest in the next generation
- **The Well Centre** — Practical support for our local community in Reading
- **Online ministry** — Live-streaming, daily devotionals, and reaching people far beyond Dawlish Road
- **Community outreach** — Events, partnerships, and presence in the life of our city
