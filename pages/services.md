## Sunday Morning Service

**Every Sunday at 10:30am**

Whether you are visiting for the first time or coming home, you are welcome. Our Sunday gathering is the heartbeat of our week — a time to worship together, hear from the Bible, pray, and encourage one another in faith.

Services run for approximately 45 to 60 minutes.

## What to Expect

When you arrive you will be greeted warmly at the door. There are no expectations and no dress code — just come as you are.

A typical Sunday morning looks like this:

- **Worship** — We sing together, accompanied by piano, violin, flute, and other instruments. Worship is a real highlight of our Sundays.
- **Teaching** — A message from the Bible, rooted in Scripture and applied to everyday life. We are currently exploring themes of character, faith, and following Jesus.
- **Prayer** — We value prayer together. There is space during the service to pray and to be prayed for.
- **Fellowship** — After the service, we gather for tea, coffee, and conversation. Some of the best moments happen here.

## Children

We love welcoming families. Children's activities run during the Sunday service so that younger ones can learn and grow in a fun, safe environment while parents engage fully in the service. Please speak to one of our team when you arrive and we will get your children settled in.

Find out more on our [Kids & Youth page]({{ '/kids-youth/' | relative_url }}).

## First Time?

If this is your first visit, do not worry — there are no expectations. Come along, have a coffee, and see what the well is all about. We would genuinely love to meet you.
