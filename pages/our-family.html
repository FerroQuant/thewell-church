Our Family

Meet the team who serve at The Well

The Well Church is led by a team of people who love Jesus and love this community. We serve together with one heart — to see lives transformed and a church family where everyone belongs.

Rod & Elsa

Lead Pastors

Rod and Elsa took on the role of pastoring The Well Church in 2022. They have a vision to reach the marginalised and see His Kingdom established — to see broken lives healed and hope restored.

Tibs & Martha

Team Leaders

Tibs and Martha serve alongside Rod and Elsa, leading the church with faithful dedication and a heart for building community.

People We Support

Missionaries and ministers we stand behind

Jenny Carroll

Evangelist

Jenny is a full-time evangelist based in Reading, serving with Mission24, Christ for All Nations (CFAN), and Open Air Campaigners (OAC). She has preached the gospel in 22 countries and leads local outreaches across Reading and beyond.
