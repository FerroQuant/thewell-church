Events

What’s happening at The Well

There is always something happening at The Well. Whether it is a Sunday service, a season of prayer, or a community event, everyone is welcome. Come along and be part of it.

  • EVERY SUN

    Sunday Morning Service

    Every Sunday at 10:30am

    The Well Church, Dawlish Road, Reading, RG2 7SD

    Our weekly gathering for worship, Bible teaching, prayer, and community. All are welcome — first-timers, regulars, and everyone in between. Services are also live-streamed on Facebook at 10:30am every week. YouTube is coming soon — stay tuned.

    Watch Live on Facebook
  • ON

    Fasting & Prayer

    Ongoing — watch Facebook for dates

    We periodically set aside seasons of fasting and prayer as a church family. Rooted in 2 Chronicles 20:1–37, we believe that when God’s people seek him together, he acts. Watch our Facebook page for announcements about upcoming fasting and prayer periods.

    Follow us on Facebook
  • DAILY

    Building Character Like Jesus — Daily Devotionals

    Posted daily on Facebook

    Our ongoing devotional series explores what it looks like to grow in the character of Jesus. Topics include Diligence, Compassion, Self-Control, and Joy. Short, practical, and rooted in Scripture — these devotionals are a wonderful way to start your day. Follow us on Facebook to receive them each morning.

    Follow the Series on Facebook
  • COMING SOON

    Reading Passion Play

    Dates to be announced

    Reading Town Centre

    The Reading Passion Play is a community event that brings the story of Jesus to the streets of Reading. The Well Church is proud to take part in this powerful and moving event alongside other churches in the city. Keep an eye on our Facebook page for details.

    Stay Updated on Facebook
